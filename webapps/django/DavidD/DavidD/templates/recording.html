A selection of jazz tunes by Trio Dieni, featuring David Dieni on drums, Dave Austin on piano and Tommy Folen on bass. The song excerpts are: Days of Wine and Roses, All the Things You Are and At Last.

Seasons of Oling is part of a multi movement fantasy series by author Ursula Le Guin and composer Elinor Armer. The full work is titled Uses of Music in Uttermost Parts and is recorded on the Koch International Label. "Uses" explores a fantastic archipelago of islands where music forms the very fabric of life. On one island, music is food. On others, water and weaving. On Oling, music is weather. The chamber group bringing Oling to life consists of cello, viola, piano, percussion and narrator (LeGuin herself). It was a great experience playing percussion in this fruitful collaboration!
